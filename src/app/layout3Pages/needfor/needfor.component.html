The growing menace of NCDs

In 2015, about 40 million deaths worldwide were caused by non - communicable diseases like Cancer, Diabetes, Chest diseases and Hypertension. Each of these diseases have some common characteristics -
  • They respond to treatment better when detected and treated in the early stages.
  • Early diagnosis and treatment plans cost less.
  • These conditions need constant monitoring by way of tests and regular consults with doctors.
Z21 solutions leverage technology to make this kind of monitoring possible remotely and constantly, thereby saving millions of lives.

Indian healthcare and its challenges

Access to quality healthcare is a challenge not only in India, but across the developing world. India especially, suffers from a huge deficit of doctors and nurses. Such challenges can only be overcome by identifying specific problems and harnessing technologies to meet them. IOT based monitoring devices, AI for diagnosis and risk profiling, networked imaging and AI guided treatment are some of the technologies that Z21 products leverage to transform Indian healthcare.

The silver lining in the COVID cloud

COVID has wreaked havoc across the world in many ways and brought to the forefront, the need for access to good quality healthcare. However, in its aftermath, there is also an increased level of acceptance of new methods of healthcare - in the form of telehealth and remote monitoring.

Z21 is responding to this change by designing and developing state-of-the art healthcare tech. products and services and making them extremely accessible.

Healthtech innovations and their way forward

Indian health-tech product companies face many challenges at both the product development stage as well as the commercialization stage. Lack of structure for idea generation and filtration, low synergy between clinicians and technologists, absence of relevant data due to lack of documentation and little or no market access for clinical testing Many of these problems can be solved if a larger overarching problem - the lack of committed involvement of medical experts throughout the product development cycle - is taken care of.

Z21 solves for this by involving senior medical experts throughout the life cycle of each of its products.
