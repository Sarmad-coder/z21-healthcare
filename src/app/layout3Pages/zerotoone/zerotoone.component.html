The genesis
& the impact

5 globally respected doctors have come together to create Z21.

They bring with them an in-depth understanding of the medical needs of the country and the on-ground realities of addressing them. They have identified specific problem areas that lend themselves to high impact solutions driven by technology.
In partnership with reputed institutions and capable, experienced technology firms, they have initiated the innovation of products and solutions to solve them. Z21 is looking at deployment for maximum impact. The deployment opportunities for pilots in different states are already in place. Each innovation is targeted to have 10x impact and touch at least a million lives!

Our
Vision

To bring about an unprecedented transformation of healthcare delivery in NCDs using emerging technologies such as AI,ML and iOT to the advantage of patients

Our
Objectives

01
To use emerging technologies such as AI, ML and IOT for sustainable, scalable solutions to important clinical problems.
02
To mentor, refine and validate the ideas of innovators by involving medical experts throughout the product life cycle.
03
To enable continuous healthcare innovation by creating a financially sustainable ecosystem for all those involved.
04
To reduce the mortality and morbidity due to noncommunicable diseases (NCDs) by using high impact med-tech innovations.
05
To make a strong social impact by ensuring that quality healthcare is more affordable, accessible and equitable.
06
To create value for investors, not only in the financial space but also with respect to a strong and lasting social impact.
