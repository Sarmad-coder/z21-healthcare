Oraican

Oral cancer detection, powered by AI
Oraican was designed and developed to address the urgent need for detection and diagnosis of oral cancer, the most common cancer in India.
Driven by a powerful AI algorithm, Oraican can help detect oral cancer early on, thereby reducing its mortality and morbidity rates and in some cases even reverse the cancer.

Oraican

The game changer - early detection with AI.
There were over 1.4 million oral cancer patients in India in 2017. About 75% of these cases were detected at advanced stages, bringing the survival rate down to about 56%. This rate can easily be improved to over 90% if the cancer is diagnosed early.
Oraican is an easy to use app. that makes screening and diagnosis of oral cancer extremely accessible and simple for users.

Oraican

Oraican puts medical help in the palm of your hand.
Oraican empowers thousands of ASHA workers, dentists and individuals with low access to medical facilities to proactively seek screening and diagnosis for oral cancer.
It also enables early detection of pre-cancer in oral cancer which lasts for 5 - 10 years. If detected in time, it can be reversed leading to several millions of rupees saved in treatment, thousands of hours of medical personnel freed up and millions of lives being saved.
