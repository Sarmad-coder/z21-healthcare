OxyNow

Oxygen saturation levels is often considered The fifth vital sign of health and wellness.
Monitoring of oxygen saturation is essential to the understanding and management of patient care - especially for patients with lung diseases and long COVID in the post COVID world.
OxyNow is a wearable IoT device that measures the oxygenation levels of the wearer and feeds the data to a mobile app. which has built-in analytics that can be shared with doctors

OxyNow

The need of the hour is smart oxygen monitoring.
Historically, the number of patients with chronic lung diseases numbers about 55 million annually.
COVID and long COVID has brought the number of people who have compromised lung function and need continuous monitoring to over 130 million. Continuous monitoring can predict a fall in saturation in advance, leading to timely intervention.
